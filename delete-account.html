Delete your account

How to permanently remove your ClassQ account and the data associated with it.

The fastest way: delete from inside the app

Open ClassQ on your phone and go to Settings → Danger zone → Delete my account.

Tapping that button deletes your account and all associated data immediately. You'll be signed out as soon as the deletion completes.

Can't access the app? Email us instead

If you no longer have the app installed or can't sign in, send an account-deletion request to the address below. Use the email that's on the account so we can verify your request.

Email support@classq.app

We'll confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, profile photo)
  • All courses you created
  • Your enrollments in other instructors' courses
  • Questions you asked
  • Responses you authored
  • Upvotes and saved questions
  • Push notification tokens for your devices
  • Any active subscription access

Aggregated analytics that don't identify you (e.g. daily active user counts) are retained. Rows where your content appeared as part of another user's activity log may still reference a generic "[deleted user]" placeholder for their consistency, but nothing that identifies you.

Before you delete: cancel any active subscription

Deleting your account does not cancel subscription billing. If you have an active Standard or Pro subscription, cancel it first:

  • iOS — apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions
  • Android — play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions

How long does deletion take?

In-app deletion is immediate. Email requests are processed within 30 days, typically much sooner.

Backups are rotated on a 30-day schedule. Your data is removed from active systems immediately and from backups within 30 days.
